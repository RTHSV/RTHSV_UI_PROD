Late. Shri. Janardan Atmaram Bhagat Saheb

He worked tirelessly and dedicated his whole life for the development and upliftment of the poor. He was always eager to provide students with educational facilities.

His dedicated and hard working life became a source of inspiration for our Hon. Chairman in establishing the Sanstha.

As the monument of his memory, the institution is named as Janardan Bhagat Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Panvel.

Late. Shri. Changu Kana Thakur Saheb (Pitajee)

Late. Shri. Changu Kana Thakur Saheb was a golden hearted person who always lived a simple life. He was a man of Principles and was a beacon light to others in the society.

He preached simple but valuable philosophy of Life. He worked very hard to educate his children and taught ethical values to them. Hon. Shri. Changu Kana Thakur Saheb was an ideal person and a real guide of Hon. Shri. Ramsheth Thakur Saheb.

Late. Matajee Bhagubai Changu Thakur (Matajee)

Late. Hon.Smt.Bhagubai Changu Thakur was a supportive lady behind the successful person Late Hon. Shri. Changu Kana Thakur. She was a continuous support as a wife and an ideal mother having a tradition of Jijau.

She taught and inculcated the importance of discipline, honesty, dedication, persistency, mannerism and sense of duty and reverence to her family members.

She also taught to defend and help the poor and weak and fight for their causes. The glory achieved by the college is an epitom of her blessings.
